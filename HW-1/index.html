Taylor Pirre


Illustrator/Artist



Bio

Taylor Pirre is a Senior-year Media Arts student attending University of Montana online from Helena, Montana. Currently slated to graduate this spring, she hopes that she will be able to combine her passion for traditional art and technology into a worthwhile career.

About Me

My favorite hobbies include creating art and playing video games. Because of this, it is my hope to one day combine my passion for creating with interactive media by developing my own virtual experiences. There is so much that can be expressed and conveyed through interactive media as an art form while, at the same time, inviting others to be a part of the artistic experience by allowing them to engage with the work.
